Input = file, Output= #s of lines, words, and bytes/characters.

1) Use argparse to accept a file_name as argument
2) Read file
3) Store count of lines, words, and characters.
3) Go through lines of file. 
	i) Lines: Split by new line '\n'. Add +1 to lines. 
	ii) Words: Split lines by spaces. Length of list = # of words in line. Add this number to words.
	iii) Bytes: Get the length of the line = # of characters. Add the number to characters.
4) Print #s of l, w, and c.
